Jam Sandwich

Description

A "jam sandwich" is a simple sandwich made with two slices of bread with a layer of jam spread between them; essentially, just bread and jam put together as a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Place the slices of bread on the plate.
  2. Spread jam on one slice of bread
  3. Put the other slice of bread on top
  4. Cut the sandwich in half or quarters
